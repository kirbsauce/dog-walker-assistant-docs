# Dog Walker Assistant

**Dog Walker Assistant (DWA)** helps volunteers at the shelter coordinate dog
walks. It pulls the live shelter roster, tracks
first walk (W1), second walk (W2), and playgroup (PG) status for every dog, and
orders the list so walkers can see at a glance who still needs attention.

For a full walkthrough — signing in, the roster, color grades, logging walks,
the WALK ORDER sort, the walk timer, and resources — see the
**[Walker Guide](WALKER.md)**.

## Open the app

Go to **[dwa.kirbsauce.com](https://dwa.kirbsauce.com)** and sign in with your
Google account. If you're new, you'll be prompted to request access; an admin
reviews and approves new walkers. Once you're approved you'll get a welcome
email, and your next sign-in lets you straight in.
